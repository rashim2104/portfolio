What if you could move thousands of people through a venue without any single spot getting dangerously packed? That was the problem at the Bharath Scouts and Guides Diamond Jubilee Jamboree in Trichy — 15,000+ attendees, and we had to keep the Global Development Village (GDV) from overcrowding.

Sairam Institutions ran the GDV, and our team built the system behind it.

## The solution
The first ask was simple: red/green buttons to block and unblock venues by capacity. We turned it into something better — a QR system where every patrol got a unique code. Scanners across 14 zones read those codes and auto-blocked a venue the moment it hit capacity. Scanners could unblock too, so flow never stalled.

## The timeline
We had 5 days, from January 22, to design, build, and test the whole thing. A few sleepless nights later, it held up — the Jamboree ran from January 28 to February 3 without the system skipping a beat.
